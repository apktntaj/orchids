## HEB GROCERY COMPANY LP

**source**: https://supplier.heb.com/requirements

**Notes**

1. Supplier link form: https://supplier.heb.com/apply-v2/supplier-type

2. All companies that want to sell products or services to H-E-B must:

- Comply with H-E-B’s Supplier Code of Conduct — basic ethical, social, and environmental expectations.
- Have a Federal Taxpayer Identification Number (TIN) — U.S. suppliers must provide this; foreign suppliers must supply a current W-8 tax certificate during setup.
- Carry liability insurance — to cover legal costs or liabilities related to products/services.
- Be prepared to provide additional documentation if requested by H-E-B’s qualification team.

3. Suppliers of food must follow U.S. food safety and regulatory laws, including:
- Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act
- Food Safety Modernization Act (FSMA)
- USDA guidelines & Country of Origin Labeling
- Food safety standards like produce safety, sanitary transport, preventive controls, and anti-adulteration efforts
